About

We are a Better Business Bureau Torch Award Finalist.

Since 1989, M.O.T. Construction has been providing quality commercial, institutional, and residential renovations as well as new construction in the greater Ottawa area. We pride ourselves in high-end, guaranteed workmanship delivered in a professional and timely manner with the customers' satisfaction always being our ultimate objective. As a business owner there is no greater satisfaction than hearing from your customers "We love the job you did for us!"

We also take great pride in being a long-time Better Business Bureau member, and the 2009 Better Business Bureau Torch Award Finalist. We look forward to continued recognition as a reliable trustworthy company in the years to come.
